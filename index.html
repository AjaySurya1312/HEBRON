Our Capabilities

Manufacturing Excellence

End-to-End Production Capabilities — from raw aluminium to finished, tested, and packaged components

01

High Pressure Die Casting

Our High Pressure Die Casting (PDC) facility operates 7 cold-chamber machines ranging from 120T to 250T clamping force, producing complex aluminium components with tight dimensional tolerances and excellent surface finish. Every shot is monitored to ensure consistent density, fill completeness and structural integrity.

120T – 250T 7 PDC Machines Cold Chamber

Equipment

  • Toyo Die Casting Machine BD-V7EX Series (350 – 800 T)
  • Technocrats 120 – 250 T Semi-Automatic (5 nos)
  • Vibro Finishing & Shot Blasting
02

Gravity Die Casting

Our Gravity Die Casting (GDC) section runs 7 heavy tilting machines supported by 3 dedicated melting furnaces and 1 holding furnace, enabling consistent alloy chemistry and temperature control. This process is ideal for larger structural automotive parts requiring superior mechanical properties and low porosity.

7 GDC Machines 3 Melting Furnaces 1 Holding Furnace

Equipment

  • Heavy Tilting GDC Machine × 7
  • Aluminium Melting Furnace × 3
  • Holding Furnace × 1
03

CNC & VMC Machining

Precision machining is performed on 5 HAAS (USA) Vertical Machining Centres and 2 CNC turning centres, complemented by 10 Auto Traub automatic lathes capable of handling stock up to 32 mm diameter. Our machining cell achieves micron-level tolerances required for shock absorber outer tube and adjuster components.

5 VMC – HAAS USA 2 CNC Turning 10 Auto Traub

Equipment

  • HAAS VMC (USA) × 5
  • CNC Turning Centre × 2 & Wire Cutting × 1
  • Auto Traub × 10 (up to 32 mm dia)
04

Powder Coating

Two semi-automatic conveyor powder coating lines deliver consistent, corrosion-resistant finishes on aluminium automotive components. Each batch is tested for Dry Film Thickness (DFT) using calibrated meters, and our Salt Spray Test (SST) chamber validates coating durability to OEM specifications — meeting the demanding environmental resistance requirements of Gabriel India and Hitachi Astemo.

2 Semi-Auto Lines DFT Verified SST Tested

Equipment

  • Semi-Auto Conveyor Coating Plant × 2
  • In-house Powder Coating Lab
  • Salt Spray Test (SST) Chamber
05

Assembly & Finishing

Our assembly section handles bush fitting, shot blasting and vibro finishing to deliver complete, ready-to-install subassemblies for our OEM customers. Bush fitting machines, hydraulic presses and shot blasting units are deployed in a lean, poka-yoke controlled flow line to ensure zero-defect output on every component dispatched.

Bush Fitting Shot Blasting Vibro Finishing

Equipment

  • Bush Fitting Machine × 3
  • 10-Ton Hydraulic Press × 4
  • Shot Blasting Machine × 1
