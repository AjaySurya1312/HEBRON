Our Story

Founded on Expertise.
Built on Trust.

Hebron Automotive Products Pvt. Ltd. was established in 2019 by Mr. N. Kumar, a seasoned industry professional with deep roots in precision manufacturing. What began as a focused venture with two high-pressure die casting machines in Hosur, Tamil Nadu, has grown into a Tier-1 certified supplier to some of India's most demanding automotive OEM supply chains.

Located strategically in Hosur — India's rapidly growing automotive hub in the Krishnagiri District — Hebron has invested steadily in people, process, and technology. Today, our facility operates 7 PDC machines, 7 GDC machines with dedicated melting furnaces, 5 HAAS VMC machining centres, a dedicated tool room, and two semi-automatic powder coating lines — all under one roof at No. 3/250, Bharathiyar Nagar.

With 120+ highly trained employees and certifications spanning IATF 16949:2016, ISO 9001:2015, and ISO 14001:2015, Hebron stands as a benchmark for quality-driven growth in the Indian automotive components sector. Our precision aluminium die cast components — shock absorber outer tubes, spring seat adjusters, eyelet assemblies, and housing sleeves — are built to exacting tolerances and delivered to Gabriel India Ltd. and Hitachi Astemo Rajasthan Brake Systems Pvt. Ltd. every day.

How We Work

Engineering-First
Philosophy

At Hebron, quality is not an afterthought — it is engineered into every stage of our production process. Our approach begins long before a part enters the die casting machine. Die design, alloy selection, process parameter validation, and first-article inspection are completed before a single production unit is approved.

Every component passes through a rigorous multi-stage inspection regime: dimensional checks on our VMM, surface roughness verification with Ra Tester, hardness and composition validation on our Spectrometer, and corrosion resistance testing in our SST Chamber. Our 5 HAAS VMC machining centres — calibrated to micron-level precision — ensure critical bore, thread, and face tolerances are consistently met.

This engineering-first philosophy is why Gabriel India Ltd. trusts Hebron to supply critical shock absorber components to their Hosur, Nashik, and Parwanoo plants simultaneously — and why Hitachi Astemo relies on us for brake system precision parts. Zero-defect delivery is not a target here; it is a cultural standard.
