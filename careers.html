Open Positions at Hebron Automotive

We are actively growing our team. If you don't see a role that fits, send us your resume anyway — we welcome talented individuals who share our commitment to precision and quality.

Production & Manufacturing

Die Casting Machine Operator

Full Time Hosur, TN ITI / Diploma

Operate High Pressure and Gravity Die Casting machines in a production environment. Monitor process parameters, ensure quality output, perform basic machine maintenance. ITI / Diploma in Mechanical/Production preferred. Experience in die casting is an advantage.

CNC / VMC Operations

CNC / VMC Machine Operator

Full Time Hosur, TN ITI / Diploma

Set up and operate HAAS VMC machining centres and CNC turning machines for aluminium automotive components. Read engineering drawings, set tools, monitor dimensions. ITI Turner/Machinist or Diploma in Mechanical Engineering.

Quality Assurance

Quality Inspector – Standard Room

Full Time Hosur, TN Diploma / BE

Perform dimensional inspection using VMM, UTM, Ra Tester, 2D measurement, and DFT Meter. Maintain inspection records, prepare quality reports, perform incoming and in-process inspection. Knowledge of IATF quality systems preferred.

Quality & Engineering

Quality Engineer – IATF / APQP

Full Time Hosur, TN BE / B.Tech

Support IATF 16949 quality system implementation, APQP, PPAP documentation, and supplier quality. Conduct internal audits, customer complaint handling, and CAPA. 2–5 years experience in automotive quality engineering required.

Maintenance

Maintenance Technician – Mechanical / Electrical

Full Time Hosur, TN ITI / Diploma

Perform preventive and breakdown maintenance on die casting machines, hydraulic systems, conveyor coating lines, and auxiliary equipment. Electrical troubleshooting, PLC basics. ITI Electrician/Fitter or Diploma in Mechanical/Electrical.

Production Management

Production Supervisor

Full Time Hosur, TN Diploma / BE

Supervise production shift operations in die casting, machining, or coating sections. Manage team of 10–20 operators, ensure daily targets, maintain quality standards, handle OEE improvement. 3–7 years experience in automotive manufacturing.

Design & Engineering

Tooling & Die Design Engineer

Full Time Hosur, TN BE / Diploma

Design and develop die casting tools, fixtures, and gauges using CAD software. Coordinate with tool room for fabrication and trials. Knowledge of ProE, SolidWorks, or AutoCAD required. Experience in die casting tooling preferred.

Supply Chain

Purchase / Supply Chain Executive

Full Time Hosur, TN Any Graduate

Manage procurement of raw materials, consumables, and spare parts. Vendor development, price negotiation, delivery coordination. Good knowledge of aluminium alloys and manufacturing consumables preferred. 1–3 years in automotive procurement.
