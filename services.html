Our Services

Six Core Manufacturing Capabilities

Every service is delivered under IATF 16949:2016 quality governance with full material traceability from incoming raw material to final dispatch.

Service 01

High Pressure Die Casting

Cold-chamber HPDC for precision aluminium parts requiring tight tolerances, good surface finish, and high volume. 7 machines from 120T to 250T, including semi-automatic Technocrats and fully automatic Toyo BD-V7EX series.

120T – 250T 7 PDC Machines Cold Chamber High Volume
Service 02

Gravity Die Casting

7 heavy tilting GDC machines with 3 dedicated melting furnaces and 1 holding furnace. Ideal for larger structural aluminium parts with superior mechanical properties, lower porosity, and consistent alloy chemistry.

7 GDC Machines 3 Melting Furnaces Low Porosity
Service 03

CNC & VMC Machining

5 HAAS USA Vertical Machining Centres, 2 CNC turning centres, and 10 Auto Traub automatic lathes (up to Ø32mm). Micron-level tolerances for critical automotive features including precision bores, threads, and profiles.

5 HAAS VMC 2 CNC Turning 10 Auto Traub
Service 04

Powder Coating

2 semi-automatic conveyor powder coating lines delivering automotive-grade finishes. All coatings verified by DFT meter and salt spray tested (SST chamber) to OEM specifications. Handles high-volume batch production consistently.

2 Conveyor Lines DFT Verified SST Tested
Service 05

Assembly & Finishing

Complete subassembly services including bush fitting (3 dedicated machines), shot blasting, vibro finishing, and hydraulic pressing (10T × 4). Deliver ready-to-install components directly to your assembly line.

Bush Fitting Shot Blasting Vibro Finishing
Service 06

Quality Inspection & Testing

Full standard room with VMM, UTM, Spectrometer, Ra Tester, 2D Master, DFT Meter, and SST Chamber. IATF 16949:2016 quality system with in-process SPC, incoming inspection, and full material traceability from heat number to dispatch.

VMM & UTM Spectrometer IATF 16949
