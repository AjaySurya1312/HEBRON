Getting Here

How to Reach Us

Hosur is well connected by road, rail, and air from Bengaluru and across South India.

By Road

Hosur is located on NH 44 (Bengaluru–Chennai National Highway), approximately 40 km from Bengaluru city centre. From Bangalore's Electronic City, take the NICE Road to Hosur Road (SH 17) and proceed towards Hosur town. The factory is located on Avalapalli Road, off Bharathiyar Nagar.

~40 km from Bengaluru

By Rail

Hosur Railway Station (station code: HSD) is approximately 4 km from our factory. Trains from Bengaluru City Junction and KSR Bengaluru run regularly to Hosur, with a journey time of about 60–75 minutes. Auto-rickshaws and taxis are available outside the station for the onward journey to Bharathiyar Nagar.

~4 km from Hosur Station

Nearest Airport

Kempegowda International Airport, Bengaluru (BLR) is the nearest major airport, approximately 45 km from our facility. Pre-booked taxis or ride-hailing services (Ola, Uber) are recommended for the journey to Hosur. Travel time is typically 50–70 minutes depending on traffic conditions on NH 44.

~45 km from BLR Airport
